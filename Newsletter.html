IEEE LAUNCHPAD KERALA 2024

About IEEE Launchpad Kerala 2024

Launchpad Kerala 2024 is a premier job fair that brings together talented individuals and innovative companies in the technical and engineering fields. This collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn aims to create meaningful connections that drive progress and innovation in Kerala's job market. The event focuses on creating opportunities for engineering students and graduates, fostering innovation, and facilitating connections between talented individuals and leading companies.

Mission

The mission of Launchpad Kerala 2024 is to bridge the gap between talented engineering students and innovative companies. It aims to create meaningful connections, foster innovation, and facilitate job opportunities in Kerala's technical job market. The vision is to empower students, promote collaboration, and drive progress in the field of technology.

Latest Updates

Important Update: Online Aptitude Test and Mock Test Details

The online aptitude test is scheduled for July 4th. Participation is mandatory for all candidates aiming to proceed to the interview round. Performance in this test, along with technical challenge results, will determine the final rank list. Interviews will be conducted in Trivandrum and Kochi based on this ranking.

Mock Test Preparation: A mock test will be conducted on July 3rd. Further details will be shared soon.

Eligibility Criteria: Only those who have completed at least one task in the Technical Challenge by July 1st will be eligible to participate in the aptitude test.

Important Dates:

  • July 1st: Deadline to start the Technical Challenge
  • July 3rd: Mock Test
  • July 4th: Online Aptitude Test

This is a crucial step towards launching your career with Launchpad Kerala. Don't miss this opportunity to showcase your skills and potential.

Roadmap

Process

Step 1: Online Registration in Launchpad Website

The candidate must register through a registration form to complete the application for Launchpad Kerala 2024.

Registration Link

Step 2: Confirmation

Candidates will receive an email from the Launchpad Kerala 2024 team within 48 hours of registration. The next steps will be communicated either through email or text messages. All updates will also be posted on the updates page of the Launchpad Kerala 2024 website.

Step 3: Join the Community

Join the GTech MuLearn Discord Server and complete the Launchpad Challenges. You can find the details here: GTech MuLearn GitHub.

Step 4: Technical Challenge

Once logged into the MuLearn Discord Server, you can find the levels and tasks related to the IEEE Launchpad category in the channel #ieee-launchpad-announcement:

  • Level 1: Foundational Skills and Introduction
  • Level 2: Specialized Learning Paths and Challenges
  • Level 3: Industry-curated Challenges and Hackathons

Submit your completed tasks on time and avail Karma points.

Step 5: Online Aptitude Test

Put your abilities to the test in our online assessment, where you'll showcase your competencies to potential employers.

Step 6: Await the Finalized Results

Stay tuned for the announcement of the finalized results. Your performance in both the Technical Challenge and the online test will be thoroughly evaluated, unlocking exciting opportunities based on your achievements.

Step 7: Final Interview

As the final stage of the selection process, gear up for the interview. This is your ultimate opportunity to showcase your skills and leave a lasting impression on recruiters. It's the final step towards securing your dream job.
